First‑Time Login (Join Your Class)

Follow these steps the first time you access Amplify Classroom.

1

Open this page in Chrome

Make sure you’re signed into your school Google account (@californiava.org) in Chrome.

2

Go to the class join link

Click the link below to open the join page:

Open Join Link
(If asked, continue in the current tab.)

3

Click “Join”

4

Sign in with Google

Choose “Continue with Google” when prompted.

5

Make sure to select your californiava.org account

If you see multiple Google accounts, pick your school account (ends with @californiava.org).
